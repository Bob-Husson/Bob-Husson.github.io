Virilla, Servant of Hippolyte, Queen of the Amazons

Dionysus uses his position as god and master to get the disguise back and prepares to go to the temple for some "nookie." But just then "Herakles" is spotted by Virilla (Anne O'Brien) who wonders where he is keeping the girdle of Hippolyte that he stole on his last trip!

Notes: The light you can see at the top of the picture shows the balcony area occupied by the orchestra. The same regulations that called for the lifeguards, a little more visible in this picture, required that the orchestra not block any fire exit. Hence a big problem with sight lines, which was solved by stationing an assistant music director (Jo McNally) at the other end of the pool to mimic the motions of the music director (Helen Porter).
