Thanaglebe and Hippodrool

Dionysus tries to persuade Xanthias to remain with him in Hades by pointing out the dangers that lurk on the outside. Note: This scene was begun with Xanthias stepping onto the 4 foot square piece of foam with Dionysus and pushing off from the side of the pool. Free-floating but tethered so it wouldn't drift too far across the pool. Both actors remained dry throughout the run!
