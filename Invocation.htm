Gods of the Theatre, Smile on Us


Richard Zajdlic (Dionysus - right), Bob Husson (Xanthias) and the Corps de (Water) Ballet (Rushmore Synchronized Swimming Club) burst into a pool of recreational swimmers who were cleared from the water by the Lifeguard's (Stage Manager) whistle. The only hint of decoration at this time was a barge at each end of the pool (brought on during the overture) and the Greek temple facade (curtains were drawn by Xanthias during opening dialogue.)
