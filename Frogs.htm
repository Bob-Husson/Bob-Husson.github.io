The Frogs (Brek-kek-kek)

Charon (Steve Pavey) and Dionysus are accosted by The Frogs (human and rubber) on the way to Hades. Choreography both on land and in the water by West End veteran Ron Howell.

Note: The human frogs began the musical number, as the lights came up, by booting a rubber frog into the pool.
