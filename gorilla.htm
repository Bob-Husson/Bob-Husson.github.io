Dionysians Greet the Travelers

The pair are serenaded by the Dionysians, singing the god's praises while unaware of his presence in their midst. They offer our heroes rides on floating gorillas.

Note: The individual dressing cubicles seen in the background were each a home to a member of the chorus who were all both frogs and Dionysians. They finished the last chord of The Frogs song with a synchronized slamming of their doors.
